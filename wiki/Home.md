# InToolz Wiki

Welcome to the InToolz wiki — your reference for everything in the app.

InToolz is a bulk management tool for Microsoft Intune and Entra ID. It lets you assign, rename, clean up, import, and export policies and profiles across one or more tenants, without clicking through the Intune portal for every item.
