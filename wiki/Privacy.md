# Privacy Policy

## Overview
This application, **InToolz**, respects your privacy. It does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your device.

## Data Usage
- The app processes your **Intune administrator account name** only while the application is running.  
- This information is used **solely in memory** to authenticate and communicate with Microsoft Graph and Intune services.  
- The account name is **not stored**, **logged**, or **transmitted** to any third party outside of Microsoft's APIs.  

## Third-Party Services
The application communicates directly with **Microsoft Graph API** as part of its intended functionality.  
All communication occurs securely using Microsoft’s official authentication and API infrastructure.

## Data Sharing
This application does **not** share data with any third parties.  
All data processing occurs locally on the user's device.

## Changes to This Policy
If changes are made to this privacy policy, the updated version will be made available through the Microsoft Store listing before the changes take effect.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please leave a comment or create an issue on this repository.
